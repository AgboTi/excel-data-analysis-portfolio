This project provides a comprehensive performance analysis of US E-commerce sales in the year 2020 using Excel. The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders understand sales trends, product performance, customer segmentation, regional profitability, and shipping efficiency.

## ğŸ¯ Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

1. **Analyze Sales and Profit Trends**  
   Understand how sales and profits varied over the months in 2020, and identify the most and least profitable periods.

2. **Evaluate Product Performance**  
   Identify which product categories and sub-categories contributed most to revenue and profit.

3. **Segment Customer Behavior**  
   Analyze how different customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) contribute to sales and profitability.

4. **Assess Regional Contribution**  
   Determine which regions are driving the most profit and which require strategic attention.

5. **Optimize Shipping Strategies**  
   Evaluate the performance of various shipping methods in terms of both cost-efficiency and profitability.

6. **Support Data-Driven Decision Making**  
   Deliver clear, visual insights through an Excel dashboard to support strategic planning and operational improvements.

## ğŸ” Key Insights

- **August** recorded the highest monthly profit, while **April** had the lowest.
- **Technology** is the top-performing category with the highest contribution to total sales.
- **Consumer** segment dominates both sales and profit share.
- **Chairs, Phones, and Machines** are the most profitable sub-categories.
- **Standard Class** shipping method yielded the highest profit.
- **Western Region** contributed the most to overall profitability, while **South and Central** regions showed minimal gains.
